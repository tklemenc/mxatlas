Email Sovereignty of Swiss Municipalities

What is this?

Email hosting map of all ~2,100 Swiss municipalities, based on automated DNS analysis (MX + SPF). See legend for provider colors.

Why does it matter?

The US CLOUD Act (2018) lets US authorities force American cloud providers to hand over data — regardless of where it's stored. A substantial amount of Swiss municipalities route official email through US infrastructure.
