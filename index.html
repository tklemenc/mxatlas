Email Sovereignty of Swiss Municipalities

What is this?

This map shows how each of Switzerland's ~2,100 municipalities hosts its official email. Municipalities are color-coded by email provider — green/blue tones indicate domestic or self-hosted infrastructure, while red/purple/amber indicate US hyperscalers subject to the CLOUD Act.

Why does it matter?

The US CLOUD Act (2018) lets US authorities force American cloud providers to hand over data — regardless of where it's stored. A substantial amount of Swiss municipalities route official email through US infrastructure, raising concerns about data sovereignty.

How is it done?

Based on automated DNS analysis of MX and SPF records for every municipality. Created by David Huser. Open source — view on GitHub. Found an error? Submit an issue.
